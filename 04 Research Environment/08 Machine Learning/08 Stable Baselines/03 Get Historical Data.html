Get some historical market data to train and test the model. For example, to get data for the different asset class ETFs during 2010 and 2023, run:

symbols = [
    qb.AddEquity("SPY", Resolution.Daily).Symbol,
    qb.AddEquity("GLD", Resolution.Daily).Symbol,
    qb.AddEquity("TLT", Resolution.Daily).Symbol,
    qb.AddEquity("USO", Resolution.Daily).Symbol,
    qb.AddEquity("UUP", Resolution.Daily).Symbol
]
df = qb.History(symbols, datetime(2010, 1, 1), datetime(2024, 1, 1))
