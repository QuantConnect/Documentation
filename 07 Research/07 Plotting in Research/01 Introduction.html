The research environment is centered around analyzing and understanding data. One way to gain a more intuitive understanding of the existing relationships in our data is to visualize it using charts. There are many different libraries which allows us to chart our data in different ways. Sometimes the right chart can illuminate an interesting relationship in the data. QuantConnect supports many of the popular chartin frameworks like matplotlib and Plotly. You can find a full list of supported libraries, including plotting libraries, in the Supported Libraries documentation.
