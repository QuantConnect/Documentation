Example 1: 100-trade Bar

We can aggregate every 100 trades to study the volatility and liquidity of a stock.

var hundredTradesConsolidator = new TickConsolidator(100);
hundred_trades_consolidator = TickConsolidator(100)

Example 2: Aggregating 10 Trade Bars

Aggregating 10 trade bars could allow bulk analysis on trading behavoir changes, desregarding the insufficient information due to shortened trading hours or holidays.

var tenBarConsolidator = new TradeBarConsolidator(10);
ten_bar_consolidator = TradeBarConsolidator(10)
