If your backtests are slow because they recalculate features and retrain models on every run, you can precompute your ML predictions in the Research Environment and save them to the Object Store. Your algorithm then streams in the precomputed predictions as a custom universe dataset, skipping all feature engineering and model inference at runtime. This pattern makes backtests significantly faster and lets you iterate on portfolio logic without rerunning the ML pipeline. For a full walkthrough, see Precomputed ML Predictions.
