The TradeStationFeeModel models the bespoke, improved fees that a QuantConnect user is given on TradeStation.

security.SetFeeModel(new TradeStationFeeModel());
security.set_fee_model(TradeStationFeeModel())

The following sections describe the trading fees of each asset class.

US Equities

US Equity trades are free for US residents. For non-US residents, they cost $5 USD per order.

Equity Options

Equity Option trades are free, (but incur $0.80c exchange fees per contract). For non-US residents, they cost an extra $5 USD per order.

Index Options

Index Option trades are free, (but incur $0.80c exchange fees per contract). For non-US residents, they cost an extra $5 USD per order.

Futures

Futures trades cost $1.75 USD per contract, or $0.50 for mini-contracts.
