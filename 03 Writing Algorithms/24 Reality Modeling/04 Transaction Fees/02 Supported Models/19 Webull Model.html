The WebullFeeModel models the fees of Webull.

security.SetFeeModel(new WebullFeeModel());
security.set_fee_model(WebullFeeModel())

The WebullFeeModel charges $0 USD for Equity and Equity Option trades. For Index Options, it charges a $0.50 USD Webull fee per contract plus a per-contract exchange fee that depends on the underlying Index (for example, SPX, SPXW, VIX, VIXW, XSP, DJX, NDX, and NDXP).
