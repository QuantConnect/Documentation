The dYdXFeeModel models the dYdX live trading fees.

security.SetFeeModel(new dYdXFeeModel());
security.set_fee_model(dYdXFeeModel())

The dYdXFeeModel models the order fees of dYdX at the lowest pricing tier for all Cryptofuture pairs, so it charges a 0.01% maker and a 0.05% taker fee.

If you add liquidity to the order book by placing a limit order that doesn't cross the spread, you pay maker fees. If you remove liquidity from the order book by placing an order that crosses the spread, you pay taker fees. dYdX adjusts your fees based on your 30-day trading volume, but we don't currently model trading volume to adjust fees.

The dYdXFeeModel charges fees in the quote currency.
