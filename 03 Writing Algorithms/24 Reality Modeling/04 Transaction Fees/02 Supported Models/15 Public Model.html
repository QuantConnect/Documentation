The PublicFeeModel models the fees of Public at the regular member tier.

security.SetFeeModel(new PublicFeeModel());
security.set_fee_model(PublicFeeModel())

The PublicFeeModel charges $0 USD for Equity trades during regular market hours and $2.99 USD per trade during extended hours. Options on stocks and ETFs are free, and Index Options cost $0.50 USD per contract. For Crypto trades, it charges a fee that depends on the order amount in USD:
