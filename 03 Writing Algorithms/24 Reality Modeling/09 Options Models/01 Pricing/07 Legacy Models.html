On February, 19th, 2026, QuantConnect announced new price models for LEAN under the OptionPriceModels namespace. The legacy models are still available under the OptionPriceModels.QuantLib namespace. The default legacy Option price model was the OptionPriceModels.QuantLib.BinomialCoxRossRubinsteinbinomial_cox_ross_rubinstein model for American Options or OptionPriceModels..QuantLib.BlackScholesblack_scholes model for European Options.

// option.PriceModel = OptionPriceModels.BinomialCoxRossRubinstein();   // New model
option.PriceModel = OptionPriceModels.QuantLib.BinomialCoxRossRubinstein();   // Legacy model
# option.price_model = OptionPriceModels.binomial_cox_ross_rubinstein()   # New model
option.price_model = OptionPriceModels.QuantLib.binomial_cox_ross_rubinstein()   # Legacy model

Certain legacy models do not have a direct equivalent in the new price models. See Supported Models for available options.

option.PriceModel = OptionPriceModels.QuantLib.CrankNicolsonFD();
option.price_model = OptionPriceModels.QuantLib.crank_nicolson_fd()
