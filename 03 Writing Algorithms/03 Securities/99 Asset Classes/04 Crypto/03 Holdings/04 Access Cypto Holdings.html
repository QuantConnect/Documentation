The CashBook stores the quantity of your Crypto holdings. To access your Crypto holdings, index the CashBook with the currency ticker. The values of the CashBook dictionary are Cash objects, which have the following properties:

var btcQuantity = Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].Amount;
var btcValue = Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].ValueInAccountCurrency;
btc_quantity = self.portfolio.cash_book['BTC'].amount
btc_value = self.portfolio.cash_book['BTC'].value_in_account_currency

To access the virtual pair of your Crypto trades, index the Portfolioportfolio object with the pair Symbol.

var securityHolding = Portfolio[_btcUsdSymbol];
security_holding  = self.portfolio[self.btc_usd_symbol]
