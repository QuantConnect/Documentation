Follow these steps to assign support seats to members in your organization:

  1. Open the Team Management page.
  2. Scroll down to the Organization Support section and then click Add Seat.
  3. On the Support page, click Select under the tier of seat that you want to assign.
  4. In the Assign seatTier Seat section, click the team member field and then select the team member that you want to assign the seat to from the drop-down menu.
  5. Click Add Seat +$price.
