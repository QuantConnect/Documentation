There are three tiers of support seats. Each tier provides different services from the QuantConnect team. The image below shows the differences between each of the support seat tiers.

Bronze seat

With a bronze seat, you can submit up to 4 support tickets per month with a QuantConnect engineer and the tickets will be responded to within 72 hours. This tier is best for quants that develop algorithms with basic compute needs and can write algorithms independently with the help of available documentation.

Silver seat

With a silver seat, you can submit up to 8 support tickets per month with a QuantConnect engineer and the tickets will be responded to within 48 hours. The silver seat unlocks access to a QuantConnect engineer who will help you find the best way to implement your project by offering suggestions on the algorithm design. This tier is best for quants or a team of quants that develop larger and more complex algorithms.

Gold seat

With a gold seat, you can submit up to 16 support tickets per month with a QuantConnect engineer and the tickets will be responded to within 24 hours. Phone support can also be provided up to 1 hour per month. The gold support tier is recommended for trading firms who need quick responses and design suggestions to keep your team operating efficiently.
