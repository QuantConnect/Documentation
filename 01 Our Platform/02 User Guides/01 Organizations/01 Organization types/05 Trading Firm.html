Trading Firm organizations have all of the features included in Team organizations and more features are included. With the Trading Firm organization, you can have an unlimited number of members and an unlimited number of collaborators simultaneously working on individual projects while keeping IP ownership of all the projects. There is no limit on the number of backtesting, research, and live trading nodes these organizations can have. Trading Firm organizations can produce 5 times the number of logs per day and per backtest as Team organizations.

The owner of a Trading Firm organization can grant various permissions to the organization’s members, including designating a member to manage the organization’s billing. Trading Firm organizations have access to custom lean builds, so they can use feature branches or historical master branches to run their strategies. Trading Firm organizations unlock access to Interactive Brokers Financial Advisor accounts to manage clients sub accounts.
