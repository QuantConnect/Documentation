FTX was founded by Sam Bankman-Fried in 2017 with the goal to "develop a platform robust enough for professional trading firms and intuitive enough for first-time users"1. FTX provides access to trading Crypto, tokenized Equities, and Bitcoin Options for clients outside of the restricted locations with no minimum deposit. FTX also provides a platform-native FTT token, an NFT marketplace, and margin borrowing services.
