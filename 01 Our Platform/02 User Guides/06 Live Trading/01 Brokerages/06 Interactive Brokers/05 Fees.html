Check the Commissions page on the IB website to see the live trading order fees. In backtests, the fees are modeled with the InteractiveBrokersFeeModel, which charges fees based on the asset class that you're trading. The fees for trading Equities, Futures, and Futures Options are:

  • Equities: $0.005/share with a $1 minimum fee
  • Futures: $1.85/contract
  • Future Options: $1.85/contract

The fees for trading Options depend on your monthly volume and the premium of the contract that you're trading. The following table shows the fees for trading Options:
