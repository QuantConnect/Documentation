The live trading performance page has a Stop button to immediately stop your algorithm from executing. Stop your algorithm if you want to perform any of the following actions:

  • Update the code files
  • Upgrade the live trading node
  • Update the settings that you entered into the deployment wizard
  • Place manual orders through your brokerage account instead of the web IDE

Furthermore, if you receive new securities in your portfolio because of a reverse merger, you also need to stop and redeploy the algorithm.

Lean actively terminates live algorithms when it detects interference outside of the algorithm's control to avoid conflicting race conditions between the owner of the account and the algorithm, so avoid manipulating your brokerage account and placing manual orders while your algorithm is running. If you need to adjust your brokerage account holdings, stop the algorithm, manually place your trades, and then redeploy the algorithm.
