Atreyu supports cash and margin accounts.

SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.Atreyu, AccountType.Cash);
SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.Atreyu, AccountType.Margin);
self.SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.Atreyu, AccountType.Cash)
self.SetBrokerageModel(BrokerageName.Atreyu, AccountType.Margin)

Create an Account

Contact Atreyu to create an Atreyu account.

Paper Trading

The Atreyu brokerage doesn't support paper trading, but you can follow these steps to simulate it:

  1. In the Initialize method of your algorithm, add one of the preceding SetBrokerageModel method calls.
  2. Deploy your algorithm with the QuantConnect Paper Trading brokerage.
