If you have a Download license, you can store datasets on your local machine. The cost of the license depends on the dataset and it's calculated on a per-file or per-day basis. You must pay for the Download license with your organization's QuantConnect Credit and you need the CLI set up to download datasets. Follow these steps to generate commands to download datasets:

  1. Open the listing page of the dataset that you want to download.
  2. Click the CLI tab.
  3. Fill in the Command Line Generator form.
  4. Select Windows or Unix.

    5. The CLI command to download the dataset displays.

To keep your local files up to date, you'll need to download the new files whenever new data becomes available.
