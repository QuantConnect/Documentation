LEAN models buying power and margin calls to ensure your algorithm stays within the margin requirements. The Oanda brokerage let's you use up to 50x leverage for margin accounts. To change the amount of leverage you can use for a Forex pair, pass a leverage argument to the AddForex method.

_symbol = AddForex("EURUSD", leverage: 35).Symbol;
self.symbol = self.AddForex("EURUSD", leverage=35).Symbol
