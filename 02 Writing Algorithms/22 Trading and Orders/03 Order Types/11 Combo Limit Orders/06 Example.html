On December 24, 2015 at 9:31 AM Eastern Time (ET), the algorithm places a combo limit order to buy one GOOG 16011SC00745000 contract and sell one GOOG 160115C00747500 contracts. By 9:36 AM, the spread drops to $1.90, which is below the limit price, so the fill model fills the combo limit order.
