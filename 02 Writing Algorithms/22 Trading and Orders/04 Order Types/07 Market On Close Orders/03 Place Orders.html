To send a market on close order, call the MarketOnCloseOrder method with a symbol and quantity. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected.

// Buy 100 shares of AAPL at the market open
buy_ticket = MarketOnCloseOrder("AAPL", 100);

// Sell 100 shares of AAPL at the market open
sell_ticket = MarketOnCloseOrder("AAPL", -100);
# Buy 100 shares of AAPL at the market open
var buyTicket = self.MarketOnCloseOrder("AAPL", 100)

# Sell 100 shares of AAPL at the market open
var sellTicket = self.MarketOnCloseOrder("AAPL", -100)

You can provide a tag and order properties to the MarketOnCloseOrder method.

MarketOnCloseOrder(symbol, quantity, tag: tag, orderProperties: orderProperties);
self.MarketOnCloseOrder(symbol, quantity, tag=tag, orderProperties=order_properties)
- By default, the latest you can place a MOC order is 15.5 minutes before the close, but some exchanges allow you to submit MOC orders closer to the market closing time. If you want to adjust the buffer required before the close, adjust the MarketOnCloseOrder.SubmissionTimeBuffer (https://github.com/QuantConnect/Lean/blob/e823dfdfb7a46cd685b58fe32590566bb4a37b9f/Algorithm.Python/MarketOnCloseOrderBufferRegressionAlgorithm.py).
