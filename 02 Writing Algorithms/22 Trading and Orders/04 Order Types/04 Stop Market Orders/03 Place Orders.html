To send a stop market order, call the StopMarketOrder method and provide a symbol, quantity, and stop price. You can provide a tag and order properties to the StopMarketOrder method. If you do not have sufficient capital for the order, it is rejected.

StopMarketOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, tag, orderProperties);
self.StopMarketOrder(symbol, quantity, stopPrice, tag, orderProperties)

To place a buy stop market order, set the stop price above the current ask price.

// When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000, buy 1 Bitcoin
var ticket = StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000);
# When Bitcoin trades up to $60,000, buy 1 Bitcoin
ticket = self.StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", 1, 60000)

To place a sell stop market order, set the stop price below the current bid price.

// When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000, sell 1 Bitcoin
var sellTicket = StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000);
# When Bitcoin trades down to $43,000, sell 1 Bitcoin
sell_ticket = self.StopMarketOrder("BTCUSD", -1, 43000)
