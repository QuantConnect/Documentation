The US Equity Short Availability dataset by Atreyu provides the available shares for open short positions in the US Equity market. The data covers 10,500 US Equities, starts in January 2018, and is delivered on a daily frequency. This dataset is created by Atreyu using information from the exchanges.

This dataset depends on the US Equity Security Master dataset because the US Equity Security Master dataset contains information on splits, dividends, and symbol changes.
