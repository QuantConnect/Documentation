It is possible to trade the continuous futures. However, the canonical symbol created by the AddFuture method is not mapped to a single underlying future contract but represents a set of rolling contracts. Thus, the prices could be frictional during a symbol-changing event. This could be catastrophic in live trading! For live trading, the orders should be placed directly on the underlying contracts. The underlying contract is available with the Mapped property. 

self.current_contract = self.continuous_contract.Mapped
self.Buy(self.current_contract, 1)

_currentContract = _continuousContract.Mapped;
Buy(_currentContract, 1);
