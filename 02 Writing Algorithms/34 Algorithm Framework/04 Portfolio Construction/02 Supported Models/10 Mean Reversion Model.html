The MeanReversionPortfolioConstructionModel implements an on-line portfolio selection technique, named "On-Line Moving Average Reversion" (OLMAR). The basic idea is to represent multi-period mean reversion as "Moving Average Reversion" (MAR), which explicitly predicts next price relatives using moving averages, and then learn portfolios by online learning techniques.

Reference:

  • Li, B., Hoi, S. C. (2012). On-line portfolio selection with moving average reversion. arXiv preprint arXiv:1206.4626. 

SetPortfolioConstruction(new MeanReversionPortfolioConstructionModel());
self.SetPortfolioConstruction(MeanReversionPortfolioConstructionModel())
