Possible applications to be tested include:

  • Using the similarity among reports to determine position sizing of securities (some examples are discussed in the paper Lazy Prices, Cohen et al. 2018 or The Positive Similarity of Company Filings and the Cross-section of Stock Returns, M. Padyšák 2020
  • Using the sentiment of the latest report to determine the portfolio allocation to give to each security in the universe
  • Using levels of uncertainty, readability, or litigious language in the report to determine position sizing of securities
