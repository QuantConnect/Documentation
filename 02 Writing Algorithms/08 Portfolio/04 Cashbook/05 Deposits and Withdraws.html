In backtests, you can add and remove cash from the cash book. To add or remove cash, call the AddAmount method. 

var newUSDBalance = Portfolio.CashBook["USD"].AddAmount(100);
var newBTCBalance = Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].AddAmount(-1.5m);
new_usd_balance = self.Portfolio.CashBook["USD"].AddAmount(100)
new_btc_balance = self.Portfolio.CashBook["BTC"].AddAmount(-1.5)

In live trading, you must manually add and withdraw cash through your brokerage account. If you adjust the cash balances in your algorithm with the AddAmount method, the cash balance in your algorithm will be out of sync with the cash balance in your brokerage account.
