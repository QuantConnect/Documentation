The recommended way to source LEAN-compatible data for local usage is by purchasing and downloading it from QuantConnect Datasets. This data is curated and maintained by QuantConnect and is easy to download with the CLI or by using the ApiDataProvider in LEAN. Before the CLI and ApiDataProvider download new files, they check if your local machine already stores the files.

Data charges are deducted from your organization's QCC balance. You can purchase QCC in the Algorithm Lab.
