To download Databento data, open a terminal in your organization workspace and then run lean data download --data-provider-historical DataBento --data-type Trade --resolution <resolution> --security-type Future --ticker <tickers> --start <YYYYMMDD> --end <YYYYMMDD> --databento-api-key <apiKey>.

$ lean data download --data-provider-historical DataBento --data-type Trade --resolution Daily --security-type Future --ticker ES --start 20240303 --end 20240404 --databento-api-key apiKey
