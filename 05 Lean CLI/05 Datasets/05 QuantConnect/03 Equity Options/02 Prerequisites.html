Before you download US Equity Options data, subscribe to the required data packages. To unlock local access to the US Equity Options dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to at least one of the following data packages:

  • US Equity Options Daily History by AlgoSeek
  • US Equity Options Hour History by AlgoSeek
  • US Equity Options Minute History by AlgoSeek

If you don't already subscribe to the US Equity Security Master by QuantConnect and US Equity Option Universe History by QuantConnect data packages, subscribe to them too. The dataset also depends on the underlying US Equities data. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.
