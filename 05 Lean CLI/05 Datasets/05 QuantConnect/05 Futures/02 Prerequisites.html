Before you download US Futures data, subscribe to the required data packages. To unlock local access to the US Futures dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to at least one of the following data packages:

  • US Futures Daily History by AlgoSeek
  • US Futures Hour History by AlgoSeek
  • US Futures Minute History by AlgoSeek
  • US Futures Second History by AlgoSeek
  • US Futures Tick History by AlgoSeek

If you don't already subscribe to the US Futures Security Master by QuantConnect and US Future Universe History by QuantConnect data packages, subscribe to them too. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.
