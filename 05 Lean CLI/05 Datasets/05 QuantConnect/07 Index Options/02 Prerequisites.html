Before you download US Index Options data, subscribe to the required data packages. To unlock local access to the US Index Options dataset, open the Pricing page of your organization and subscribe to at least one of the following data packages:

  • US Index Options Daily Updates by AlgoSeek
  • US Index Options Hour History by AlgoSeek
  • US Index Options Minute History by AlgoSeek

If you don't already subscribe to the US Index Option Universe by QuantConnect data package, subscribe to it too. You need billing permissions to change the organization's subscriptions.
