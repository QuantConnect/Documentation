sm(symbol, bollinger_period=20, bollinger_multiplier=2.0, keltner_period=20, keltner_multiplier=1.5, resolution=None, selector=None)[source]

Creates a Squeeze Momentum indicator to identify market squeezes and potential breakouts. Compares Bollinger Bands and Keltner Channels to signal low or high volatility periods.

Parameters:
  • symbol (Symbol) — The symbol whose Stochastic RSI we seek
  • bollinger_period (int, optional) — The period of the relative strength index
  • bollinger_multiplier (float, optional) — The period of the stochastic indicator
  • keltner_period (int, optional) — The smoothing period of K output
  • keltner_multiplier (float, optional) — The smoothing period of D output
  • resolution (Resolution, optional) — The type of moving average to be used
  • selector (Callable[IBaseData, IBaseDataBar], optional) — The resolution
Returns:

A StochasticRelativeStrengthIndex configured with the specified periods and moving average type

Return type:

SqueezeMomentum
