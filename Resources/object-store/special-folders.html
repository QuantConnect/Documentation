Your organization's Object Store includes a reserved .assistant folder that the QuantConnect AI assistants read. It holds the custom skills, memories, and templates you define for your organization, which let the assistants apply your own expertise, remember your preferences, and start new projects from your own scaffolds.

The .assistant folder contains the following subfolders:

SubfolderPurpose
skills Custom Skills — reusable instructions and conventions the assistants apply.
memories Custom Memories — facts the assistants remember across conversations.
templates Custom Templates — your own project scaffolds to start new projects from.
