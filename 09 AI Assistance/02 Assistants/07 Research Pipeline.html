You can assign each assistant to a single stage of the Research Pipeline. When a card lands in that stage on the Research Pipeline, the assigned assistant automatically deploys and starts working on the project. In the assistant configuration, you can choose which stage to assign the assistant to, or choose to leave it unassigned and just deploy it manually when you want it to work. The predefined Assistants come with their stage already set. For example, the Backtest Assistant is assigned to the Backtest stage of the Research Pipeline.
