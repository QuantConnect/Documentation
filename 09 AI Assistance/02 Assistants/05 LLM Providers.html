By default, Assistants run using our free QuantConnect Meta Agent. It requires no setup and is the right choice when you want the assistant working without bringing your own LLM API key.

Organizations on the Trading Firm or Institution tier can supply an API key from an LLM vendor of their choice, such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or a self-hosted endpoint. With your own provider, you can configure the reasoning effort, the maximum number of turns the assistant takes per deployment, and the tool selection policy. Use this option when you want to swap in a specific model, tune those parameters, or route costs through your own account.
