Organizations on the Trading Firm or Institution tier can schedule assistant tasks to run using the same Scheduled Event system that you use in QuantConnect algorithms, including market open, fifteen minutes before close, the first trading day of the month, and more. Since Scheduled Events are aware of the market hours, you can ensure your assistants won't run on weekends or US market holidays if that's your intention. This saves you from maintaining a holiday calendar or weekday filter in your prompts.
