The Conductor coordinates six specialized assistants rather than picking up the tools itself. The Ideas Assistant keeps the Research Pipeline stocked by sourcing fresh, testable strategy ideas from recent financial news and trading blog posts. The Research Assistant is the default starting point on a new idea and runs statistical validation inside a notebook, then the Research Validation Assistant pressure-tests the statistical properties and underlying logic. From there, the Backtesting Assistant turns the validated idea into a QuantConnect algorithm. The Paper Testing Assistant deploys the result and watches for divergence and performance drift as it heads toward consideration for live capital. Once a strategy is live, the Live Monitoring Assistant keeps an eye on the active positions and flags material adverse events that genuinely need your attention.
