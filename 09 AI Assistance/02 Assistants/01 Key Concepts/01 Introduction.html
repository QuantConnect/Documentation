Assistants are specialized AI agents inside QuantConnect that handle the distinct stages of building a trading strategy — sourcing ideas, validating them, coding the backtest, and shepherding the result through paper trading. Each one is an expert in a single part of the workflow, with the tools to actually do the work rather than just describe it. Talk to an assistant directly, or hand the whole job to the Conductor and let it route the work through the team on your behalf.
