To keep community strategies reliable, readable, and easy to maintain, follow these guidelines when submitting or updating strategies:

  • Ensure backtests run without buying power or runtime errors.
  • Use current QuantConnect LEAN APIs and patterns; avoid deprecated methods.
  • Match scheduling cadence to data resolution (daily vs intraday vs monthly).
  • Use a single self.set_start_date(self.end_date - timedelta(5*365)) backtest period.
  • Follow PEP8-style readability with a maximum line length of 120 characters.
  • Add a leading underscore to private class members.
  • Keep total backtest runtime under 1 hour.
  • Use the default transaction fee model. Do not override fee models. See Transaction Fee Modeling – Key Concepts .
  • Strategies must not add or modify cash balances during a backtest (no cash deposits or withdrawals). Initial capital must be set only via self.set_cash(). See CashBook – Portfolio Cash Management .
