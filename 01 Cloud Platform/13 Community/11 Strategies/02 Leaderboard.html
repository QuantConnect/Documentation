The Leaderboard lists top performing strategies ranked by performance metrics.

The strategies on the Leaderboard are ranked by their out-of-sample score over the last three months. The score is the Sharpe ratio over one year. Strategies with less than one year are given a penalty to their score equivalent to the fraction of the year they have been in-of-sample. For examples, a strategy that is in-of-sample for six months will have its Sharpe ratio halved to calculate its score.
